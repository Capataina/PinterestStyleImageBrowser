# RustConf 2024 — WASM + no_std Workshop + General Programme

## Source reference

- Programme: https://2024.rustconf.com/programs/
- Trip report (Red Hat): https://developers.redhat.com/blog/2024/11/27/rustconf-2024-trip-report

## Claim summary

RustConf 2024 included a WebAssembly + no_std workshop showing Rust on browsers and embedded devices. General talks covered security, performance, ecosystem growth across game-dev, robotics, space, 3D. RustConf 2026 in Montreal (Sep 8-11) will follow.

## Relevance to our project

A1 + A3: RustConf is the largest annual Rust gathering. Tauri + WASM + ML adjacency is increasingly visible. The audiences (Rust core team + working groups + production users) overlap directly with A1 and A3.

## Specific takeaways

- Rust-on-the-web (Tauri + WebView, but also Leptos/Yew + WASM) is a recurring track.
- A future portfolio direction: contribute a talk on "Building a Tauri+ORT+CLIP desktop app" to RustConf or a regional Rust conf.
